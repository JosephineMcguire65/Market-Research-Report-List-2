<p><p>Catheter stabilization devices, also known as catheter securement devices, come in various types tailored for different applications. Arterial securement devices are designed to anchor arterial catheters, ensuring stability during procedures. Central venous catheter securement devices stabilize central venous catheters, preventing movement and potential complications. Peripheral securement devices are used for peripheral catheters, providing support and minimizing the risk of dislodgment. Urinary securement devices are specifically designed for urinary catheters, promoting safety and comfort while preventing accidental removal or leakage.</p></p>
