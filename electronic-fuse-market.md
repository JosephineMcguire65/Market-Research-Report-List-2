<p><p>An electronic fuse, or e-fuse, is a semiconductor-based device designed to protect electrical circuits from overcurrent conditions. Unlike traditional fuses that require replacement after a single-use, electronic fuses can reset themselves, providing a more efficient and reliable solution for circuit protection. They are widely used in various sectors including automotive, consumer electronics, and renewable energy systems, owing to their enhanced performance and compact size.</p><p>The Electronic Fuse Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 11% during the forecast period.
