<p><p>Real Time Kinematic (RTK) systems are advanced satellite navigation techniques used to enhance the precision of location data. They operate using a fixed base station and a mobile receiver, providing centimeter-level accuracy in real-time by correcting satellite signals. This technology is widely utilized in various applications, including agriculture, construction, surveying, and autonomous vehicles, where high precision is crucial.</p><p>The Real Time Kinematic (RTK) System Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 5.9% during the forecast period, driven by increasing demand for accurate positioning solutions across industries. Moreover, the push towards automation in agriculture through precision farming is creating significant opportunities for RTK solutions. Technological advancements, such as the integration of RTK with other positioning technologies like the Global Navigation Satellite System (GNSS), are also contributing to market growth.
