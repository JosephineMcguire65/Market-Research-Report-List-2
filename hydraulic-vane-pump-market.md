<p><p>The hydraulic vane pump market includes two main types: variable pumps and quantitative pumps. Variable pumps adjust their displacement according to system demands, allowing for efficient flow control and energy savings. They are ideal for applications requiring variable speed and pressure. In contrast, quantitative pumps deliver a fixed flow rate regardless of demand, ensuring consistent performance in systems that need stable output. Both types serve different operational needs, contributing to diverse applications in hydraulics across various industries.</p></p>
