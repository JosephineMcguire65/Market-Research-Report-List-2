<p><p>Bipolar Electrode (BPE) technology is used in electrochemical applications, enabling efficient electron transfer between electrodes. The market can be categorized into two main types: straight and curved bipolar electrodes. Straight BPEs are characterized by a linear design, optimizing space and current distribution, making them suitable for planar setups. Curved BPEs, on the other hand, feature a contoured design that enhances contact with various surface geometries, providing improved performance in applications requiring complex shapes. Each type caters to specific operational needs and efficiency requirements.</p></p>
