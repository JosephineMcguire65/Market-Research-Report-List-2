<p><p>The immunoassay instruments and analyzers market comprises several types. Chemiluminescence immunoassays use light emission for detection, offering high sensitivity. Immunofluorescence analyzers utilize fluorescent labels to visualize the presence of analytes. Radioimmunoassays leverage radioisotopes for quantification but are declining due to safety concerns. Enzyme-linked immunoassays employ enzyme-substrate reactions for detection, making them popular for various applications. The consumables market includes necessary reagents, kits, and accessories that support these diagnostic methods, ensuring consistent performance and reliability in testing.</p></p>
