<p><ul><li>Twin-tube</li><li>Mono-tube</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The shock absorber and strut assembly market features two primary types: twin-tube and mono-tube systems. Twin-tube shock absorbers consist of an inner and outer tube, providing a smooth ride and efficient damping, making them popular in everyday vehicles. Conversely, mono-tube systems integrate the piston and pressure chamber into a single tube, offering superior heat dissipation and responsiveness, often preferred in performance and off-road applications. Each type serves specific suspension needs and preferences, influencing vehicle handling and comfort.</p></p>
