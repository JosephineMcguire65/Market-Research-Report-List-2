<p><p>The mesh nebulizer market is divided into two main types: static mesh nebulizers and vibrating mesh nebulizers. Static mesh nebulizers use a fixed mesh to create a fine aerosol spray, suitable for various medications, while vibrating mesh nebulizers employ a piezoelectric element to vibrate the mesh, producing a uniform mist more efficiently. Both types are designed for effective drug delivery in respiratory treatments, offering benefits such as portability and reduced treatment time, catering to diverse patient needs.</p></p>
