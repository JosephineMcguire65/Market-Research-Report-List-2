<p><ul><li>High Alkali Cloth</li><li>Alkali Cloth</li><li>Alkali Free Cloth</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The fiberglass cloth market is characterized by three primary types: High Alkali Cloth, Alkali Cloth, and Alkali-Free Cloth. High Alkali Cloth contains higher levels of alkaline materials, making it suitable for cost-effective applications but less durable against moisture. Alkali Cloth has a moderate alkali content, offering a balance of performance and cost, often used in construction and automotive sectors. Alkali-Free Cloth is designed for superior performance, resisting moisture and chemicals, making it ideal for high-end applications such as aerospace and marine industries.</p></p>
