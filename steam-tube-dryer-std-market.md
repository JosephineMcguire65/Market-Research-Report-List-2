<p><p>A Steam Tube Dryer (STD) is a thermal drying system that utilizes steam to efficiently remove moisture from materials. The market for STD can be categorized into three types: automatic, semi-automatic, and manual. Automatic STDs operate with minimal human intervention, optimizing energy and time efficiency, while semi-automatic systems combine automated processes with manual controls, offering flexibility. Manual STDs require complete operator involvement, providing greater control but often at the cost of efficiency. Each type caters to different operational needs and industry preferences.</p></p>
