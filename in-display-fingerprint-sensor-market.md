<p><strong>The In-Display Fingerprint Sensor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Optical In-Display Fingerprint Sensor</li><li>Ultrasonic In-Display Fingerprint Sensor</li><li>Capacitive In-Display Fingerprint Sensor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The In-Display Fingerprint Sensor market is categorized into three main types: </p><p>1. **Optical In-Display Fingerprint Sensors** use light to capture fingerprint images, offering high accuracy and cost-effectiveness, commonly found in smartphones. </p><p>2. **Ultrasonic In-Display Fingerprint Sensors** employ ultrasonic waves to create detailed 3D images of fingerprints, providing enhanced security and performance in diverse conditions. </p><p>3. **Capacitive In-Display Fingerprint Sensors** detect the electrical capacitance changes when a finger touches the screen, delivering reliable performance and quick response times. Each type addresses varying consumer needs and preferences.</p></p>
