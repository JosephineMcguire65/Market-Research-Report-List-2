<p><strong>The Emergency Shut-off Valve Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Motorised Valve</li><li>Solenoid Valve</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Emergency Shut-off Valve market comprises two main types: motorised valves and solenoid valves. Motorised valves utilize electric motors for precise control, enabling rapid response to emergencies by shutting off fluid flow automatically or manually. Solenoid valves, on the other hand, use electromagnetic coils to operate, providing quick and reliable shut-off capabilities. Both valve types are essential for enhancing safety in industrial processes, preventing catastrophic failures, and ensuring compliance with regulations by mitigating risks in hazardous situations.</p></p>
