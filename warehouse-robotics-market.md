<p><p>The warehouse robotics market comprises two main types: mobile robotics and fixed robotics. Mobile robotics refers to autonomous robots that navigate and transport goods throughout the warehouse environment, enhancing flexibility and reducing labor costs. These robots can adapt to changing layouts and obstacles. In contrast, fixed robotics involves stationary systems, such as conveyor belts and robotic arms, designed for specific tasks like sorting or palletizing. Both types optimize warehouse operations, improve efficiency, and streamline logistics processes.</p></p>
