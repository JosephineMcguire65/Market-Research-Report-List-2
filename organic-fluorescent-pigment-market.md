<p><p>The Organic Fluorescent Pigment market is categorized into three main types: Thermoset Type, Thermoplastic Type, and Other (Aqueous Dispersions). Thermoset Type pigments are designed for applications requiring robust, permanent finishes, often used in coatings and plastics. Thermoplastic Type pigments remain flexible and can be remolded, ideal for applications involving heat or deformation. Aqueous Dispersions consist of pigments dispersed in water, offering eco-friendly solutions suitable for ink and paint formulations, promoting safer processing and reduced environmental impact.</p></p>
