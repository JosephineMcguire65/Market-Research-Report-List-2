<p><strong>The Brushless AC Motor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Inner Rotor Brushless AC Motors</li><li>Outer Rotor Brushless AC Motors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The brushless AC motor market is divided primarily into inner rotor and outer rotor configurations. Inner rotor brushless AC motors have the rotor mounted inside the stator, offering compact designs and high torque density, making them suitable for applications like robotics and aerospace. Conversely, outer rotor brushless AC motors feature the rotor surrounding the stator, providing advantages in cooling and torque generation at lower speeds, ideal for applications such as fans and electric vehicles. Both types enhance efficiency and performance.</p></p>
