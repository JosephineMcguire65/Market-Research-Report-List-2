<p><p>The automobile fluid pipe products market can be categorized into flexible and rigid types. Flexible pipes are made from materials that allow bending and movement, making them suitable for applications where space is limited or where vibrations occur. They offer advantages like easy installation and reduced risk of breakage. Rigid pipes, on the other hand, are constructed from harder materials, providing durability and stability in high-pressure environments. Both types serve essential roles in transporting fluids like fuel, coolant, and oil within vehicles.</p></p>
