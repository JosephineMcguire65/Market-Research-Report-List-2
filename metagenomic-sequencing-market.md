<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Metagenomic Sequencing Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Shotgun Metagenomic Sequencing</li><li>16S rRNA Sequencing</li><li>Metatranscriptomics</li><li>Whole-genome Sequencing & De Novo Assembly</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The metagenomic sequencing market includes several key types: </p><p>1. **Shotgun Metagenomic Sequencing** allows for whole-genome analysis of microbial communities, providing a comprehensive view of genetic material.</p><p>2. **16S rRNA Sequencing** is targeted at profiling bacterial communities by amplifying the 16S ribosomal RNA gene, emphasizing taxonomic identification.</p><p>3. **Metatranscriptomics** focuses on capturing active gene expression within microbial communities.</p><p>4. **Whole-genome Sequencing** provides complete genetic information about individual organisms.</p><p>5. **De Novo Assembly** involves reconstructing genomes from short DNA sequences, enabling the discovery of new species and genetic elements.</p></p>
