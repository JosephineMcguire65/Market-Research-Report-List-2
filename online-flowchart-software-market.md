<p><p>Online flowchart software can be categorized into two primary market types: cloud-based and on-premises. Cloud-based solutions are hosted on remote servers, allowing users to access and collaborate on flowcharts from any device with internet connectivity. This model offers scalability and eliminates the need for extensive IT infrastructure. In contrast, on-premises software is installed locally on a user's computer or server, providing greater control over data security and customization but often requiring more maintenance and higher upfront costs.</p></p>
