<p><strong>The Automotive Lidar Sensor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Solid State Lidar</li><li>Mechanical/Scanning Lidar</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive LiDAR sensor market is primarily divided into two types: solid-state LiDAR and mechanical/scanning LiDAR. Solid-state LiDAR utilizes fixed components with no moving parts, offering higher durability and lower production costs, making it suitable for mass-market applications. In contrast, mechanical/scanning LiDAR employs rotating components to capture a 360-degree view, providing higher accuracy and detailed imaging but typically at a higher cost and complexity. Both technologies are crucial for advancing autonomous vehicle capabilities and enhancing road safety.</p></p>
