<p><p>The paint and coating stripper market comprises three main types: solvent-based, caustic-based, and acidic-based strippers. Solvent types use organic solvents to dissolve paints, making them effective yet often volatile. Caustic types utilize alkaline substances to break down organic materials, suitable for heavy-duty applications. Acidic types employ strong acids to remove rust and paint, effective on metal surfaces but requiring careful handling. Each type caters to specific removal needs, balancing effectiveness, safety, and environmental considerations.</p></p>
